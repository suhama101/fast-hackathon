Cybersecurity Deployment RFP

1. The bidder must provide a complete cybersecurity assessment and deployment plan.
2. The bidder must support endpoint protection, SIEM, log retention, and incident response integration.
3. The bidder shall deploy network segmentation controls across all ministry sites.
4. The bidder must provide vulnerability scanning and remediation guidance.
5. The bidder shall submit a security architecture diagram and implementation roadmap.
6. The bidder must provide 24/7 monitoring and alert triage.
7. The bidder must guarantee critical incident response within 30 minutes.
8. The bidder must provide monthly security reporting and executive dashboards.
9. The bidder shall maintain encryption for data in transit and at rest.
10. The bidder must comply with ISO 27001, NIST CSF, or equivalent security standards.
11. The bidder must provide named security lead and project manager resources.
12. The bidder shall complete user awareness training for all designated staff.
13. The bidder must provide access control, MFA, and privileged access governance.
14. The bidder must document backup, recovery, and disaster recovery procedures.
15. The bidder shall support secure cloud configuration and hardening.
16. The bidder must provide change management and audit log procedures.
17. The bidder must provide asset inventory and endpoint coverage reporting.
18. The bidder shall maintain confidentiality, data protection, and escalation protocols.
19. The bidder must submit all deliverables before the implementation deadline.
20. The bidder must include compliance certification, implementation evidence, and support SLAs.

Submission deadline: 15 December 2026 at 3:00 PM.
Questions must be submitted by 5 December 2026.
The successful bidder shall support a 12-month managed security operations period.
