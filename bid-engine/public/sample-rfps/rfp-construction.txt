Request for Quotation - Office Building Renovation
Organization: CUST University Islamabad
Reference: CUST-RENOV-2026-04

1. Background and Objective
CUST University Islamabad invites sealed quotations from qualified construction
firms for the renovation of an academic office building located on the main
campus. The work includes civil repairs, electrical upgrades, HVAC improvements,
accessibility enhancements, fire safety upgrades, interior finishes, and limited
landscaping around the renovated facility.

The purpose of this RFQ is to select a contractor capable of completing the work
within an active university environment while maintaining safety, schedule
discipline, and high workmanship standards. Classes and administrative operations
will continue during the renovation period, so the contractor must provide a
phased execution plan with noise, dust, and access controls.

2. Scope of Work
The successful bidder shall perform:
- Demolition and removal of damaged partitions, ceiling panels, flooring, and fixtures.
- Repair of plaster, masonry, waterproofing, and minor structural defects.
- Installation of new flooring, ceiling grid, doors, paint, and interior finishes.
- Electrical rewiring for lighting, power sockets, panels, emergency lighting, and backup circuits.
- HVAC servicing, duct cleaning, replacement of selected units, and thermostat controls.
- Fire alarm, extinguishers, signage, emergency exit lights, and evacuation route markings.
- Wheelchair ramps, accessible washroom improvements, and handrail installation.
- Site cleanup, debris disposal, testing, commissioning, and handover documentation.

3. Mandatory Requirements
- Contractor must be registered with Pakistan Engineering Council in an appropriate category.
- Contractor must have completed at least three building renovation projects in the last five years.
- Site supervisor and safety officer must be assigned full time during active work.
- Contractor must submit a detailed work schedule and phased access plan.
- Contractor must comply with OSHA-style safety procedures and local building regulations.
- All electrical work must be supervised by qualified electrical personnel.
- Materials must meet approved specifications and be supported by manufacturer data sheets.
- Work must include warranty for workmanship and installed equipment.
- Contractor must maintain public liability insurance and workers compensation coverage.

4. Evaluation Criteria
Quotations will be evaluated using the following weights:
- Technical compliance with scope and specifications: 30 percent
- Relevant renovation experience and references: 25 percent
- Project schedule, phasing, and campus safety plan: 20 percent
- Quality of proposed materials and warranty terms: 10 percent
- Financial quotation and value for money: 15 percent

5. Budget
The estimated budget range for this renovation is PKR 18M to PKR 28M inclusive
of labor, materials, equipment, testing, documentation, and applicable taxes.
Any exclusions must be clearly identified in the financial quotation.

6. Submission Deadline
Quotations must be submitted to the Procurement Office, CUST University
Islamabad, by 20 September 2026 at 2:00 PM Pakistan Standard Time. A mandatory
site visit will be held on 5 September 2026 at 11:00 AM. Requests for
clarification must be received by 10 September 2026.

7. Certifications and Compliance Clauses
- Pakistan Engineering Council registration.
- Tax registration and active taxpayer status.
- Safety method statement and environmental management plan.
- Compliance with campus security, fire safety, labor law, and municipal
  construction requirements.
- Non-collusion declaration and acceptance of university procurement terms.

8. Questions to Answer
1. Describe your proposed construction methodology, work sequencing, and phasing
   approach for an occupied university campus.
2. Provide details of four comparable renovation projects, including client,
   value, duration, and completion certificate status.
3. Explain your safety plan for students, staff, visitors, workers, dust control,
   noise control, and emergency access.
4. Provide your material specifications, warranty coverage, project schedule,
   and itemized financial quotation.

9. Submission Format
The quotation package must include company profile, PEC registration, tax
documents, project references, method statement, safety plan, work schedule,
material schedule, itemized bill of quantities, warranty terms, and signed forms.
Incomplete quotation packages may be rejected without further evaluation.
